The Problem

Students already have ChatGPT on their phones. Banning it is theater.

The AI they're using was designed to answer, not to teach. It short-circuits the productive struggle that learning requires.

No existing product gives schools a sanctioned, pedagogically sound AI environment at the device level. Until now.

What We Offer

Three layers.
One package.

01

Hardware Bundle

Pre-configured MacBook Neo, arriving ready from day one.

Enrolled in your MDM profile on arrival. Competing AI tools blocked at device and DNS level via Jamf and Cloudflare. First managed education environment built on this hardware. No IT burden on your end.

02

Managed AI Environment

A school-specific AI tutor that scaffolds learning instead of replacing it.

Your own ChatGPT organization with a custom Socratic system prompt. The AI asks before it answers — prompting students to explain their thinking, identify where they're stuck, and work through problems rather than around them.
